REQ-ID: PRD_NFR_001
Description: The product shall ensure that tickets are not returned for the wrong event
Type: Usability and humanity (10)
Rationale: Customers could have tickets for more than one event, and could inadvertently cancel some tickets for the wrong one.
This needs to be avoided to avoid possible administrative costs.
Fit Criterion: 95% of customers who request to return tickets during the transaction do not attempt to cancel the request after the transaction.
